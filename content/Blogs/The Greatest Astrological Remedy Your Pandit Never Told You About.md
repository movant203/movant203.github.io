### Introduction
Let's address you, the reader, first. Maybe you come from a typical Hindu family, in which case, you probably do have a home village even if you are living in the city and there's a good chance that there's a traditional pandit your family consults from time to time there (or elsewhere). Even if you don't know about your Village, there's a good chance that your family consulted some pandit or traditional astrologer sometime. Maybe you are not a Hindu, but you still do believe in Astrology and have consulted "Vedic" Astrology - I prefer the term "jyotisa" - sometime. Maybe you are interested in spiritual practices. Maybe you are interested in occult. Maybe you are feeling troubled in life. Maybe you clicked on this blog just because, this is no mere coincidence let me assure you. If you fall into any of these categories, well you are at the right place and even if you don't believe you do, well you are in the right place anyway!

Let's address what's an Astrological Remedy now. It's generally a task, Puja, donation and a change in some habit that is suggested as a remedial measure by an astrologer in case of an unfavorable condition in the Kundli - it could be the ongoing dasha, some graha badha, graha dosha, health issues or maybe just a desire of the concerned. How do remedies work? That's a question for another blog, but the gist of remedies is that you are supposed to get favourable results or at least decrease the intensity of the suffering/burden once you perform them.

I, as a student of jyotisa, personally don't believe in most remedies that you may find in books and hear from typical astrologers who merely practice it as business. However, there are many that are practical and some, obscure (and still give results everytime, even I don't understand the link between them and the actual problem). However, I feel that there's one remedy, or let's call it a practice instead, that is often neglected or just not discussed. There's a good reason as to why that's the case, as you might understand later.

Now what's the greatest astrological remedy am I talking about? Well, it's not an "astrological remedy" per se but it's a practice, a constant regular practice, a spiritual practice - Sadhana.
